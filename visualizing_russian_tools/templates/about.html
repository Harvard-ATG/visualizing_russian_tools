About the project

What is Visualizing Russian?

Visualizing Russian is a suite of web-based tools for language learners, researchers, and teachers created by Steven Clancy.

Articles

Clancy, Steven J. and Lee, Paige (2022) "Visualizing Russian: Illuminating Corpora, Conjugations, and Classrooms," Russian Language Journal: Vol. 72: Iss. 1, Article 2.
Available at: https://scholarsarchive.byu.edu/rlj/vol72/iss1/2

Handouts

Visualizing Russian: Teaching Vocabulary at the Intersection of Frequency, Grammar, and Communication (2021).
